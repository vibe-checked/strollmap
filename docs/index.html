Support

How can we help?

Find answers to common questions below. If you can't find what you're looking for, reach out directly — we typically respond within one business day.

How do I book a walking tour?
Tap any tour card on the Browse tab to open its detail page. Scroll down and tap "Request this tour". Choose an available slot, set your party size, add an optional note to the guide, and tap "Send request". Your booking will appear on the Bookings tab immediately.
How do I cancel or modify a booking?
Go to the Bookings tab and tap the booking you want to change. From the detail sheet you can pick a different available slot, adjust your party size, update your note, or cancel the booking entirely.
What does "pending" mean on my booking?
Pending means your request has been sent and is waiting for the guide to confirm. Once the guide accepts, the status changes to Confirmed. If the guide is unable to accommodate you, it will show as Declined and will move to your Past bookings.
How do I become a guide and create a tour?
Tap the "I'm a tourist" pill in the top-right corner to switch to Guide mode. From the My tours tab, tap "+ Create a new tour". Fill in the title, city, description, meeting point, available slots, and at least one itinerary stop, then tap Save. Your listing will appear in Browse immediately.
How do I respond to booking requests as a guide?
Switch to Guide mode and open the Inbox tab. Pending requests appear at the top — tap Confirm or Decline for each one. Handled requests move to the Handled section below.
Are the tours really free?
Yes. All tours on Strollmap are free to join. Guides offer their time on a tip-based model — if you enjoyed your walk, a tip is appreciated but never required. There are no booking fees or in-app purchases.
Why isn't my data syncing to another device?
Strollmap is a local-first app — all your tours, bookings, and profile data are stored on your device only and do not sync to any cloud server. To move data to a new device, you will need to re-enter it. This is by design to protect your privacy.
How do I delete my data?
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can remove it completely by uninstalling the app. This permanently deletes your profile, tours, and bookings. There is no account to close because no data is sent to our servers.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy, simply explained

Last updated: June 1, 2026. This policy applies to the Strollmap iOS and Android applications published by Mark Utility Labs.

🔒

Short version: Strollmap stores everything on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. There are no accounts, no servers, and no third-party analytics.

1. Information we collect

Strollmap does not collect any personal information. All data you enter — your profile name, avatar, bio, tours you create, and bookings you make — is stored exclusively on your device using the operating system's local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device.

2. Information we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry
  • Payment information of any kind
  • Photos, contacts, or any other device data

3. Local storage only

All app content — your profile, tours, bookings, and settings — is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage, a standard on-device key-value store. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data. We have no ability to access, recover, or transfer this data.

4. Third-party services

Strollmap does not integrate any third-party analytics services (such as Firebase, Mixpanel, or Amplitude), advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or social login providers. No SDK or library in the app contacts an external server on your behalf.

5. Permissions

Strollmap does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, photo library, or any other system permission. The only system capability used is local storage, which requires no special permission.

6. Children's privacy

Strollmap does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Because the app collects no personal data at all, it poses no privacy risk to users of any age. If you believe a child has provided personal data through another channel, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

7. Data security

Because no data is transmitted to external servers, the security of your information depends solely on your device's own security features (such as passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID). We recommend keeping your device and operating system up to date.

8. Changes to this policy

If we ever change how data is handled — for example, if a future version introduces optional cloud sync — we will update this policy and notify users through an in-app notice and a new App Store release note before those changes take effect.
